Our Company

Construction Outfitters International is a full service construction-based corporation established to provide construction services, project management, engineering support, logistics services, FSO Services and facilities maintenance for a market within the public and private sectors. COI is structured and staffed to satisfy a wide range of government and commercial facility construction and support requirements. These services include program planning, construction management, facility maintenance, systems installation and maintenance, logistics, and construction security support.
